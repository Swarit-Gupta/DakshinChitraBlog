A living museum of South Indian art, architecture, and culture — captured during our college trip.

The Craft Bazaar

Chennai — April 2025 • College Trip

Our first stop was the craft bazaar, which exhibits and sells artefacts and souvenirs made by traditional craftsmen. It was full of colourful stalls displaying handicrafts, handmade bags and mats, paintings, and handmade jewellery.

Exploring Heritage Houses

Heritage Trail • Tamil Nadu

We explored traditional houses from Tamil Nadu, Kerala, Karnataka, and Andhra Pradesh. Each home revealed centuries of design adapted to climate and lifestyle.

The Journey Begins

Travel Notes • Bus Ride

We started our journey early in the morning in our college bus, filled with excitement and laughter. After a pleasant drive, we reached Dakshin Chitra, a well-known heritage center.

Gratitude

At last, I would like to express my heartfelt thanks to our professor for arranging this wonderful trip and giving us such a memorable experience outside the classroom. It was truly a day filled with joy, learning, and unforgettable memories that we will cherish for a long time.
